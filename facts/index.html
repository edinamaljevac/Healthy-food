Interesting facts

Ingredients that contain Vitamin A

Vitamin A is an important fat-soluble vitamin. It comes in two forms: retinol, which is primarily found in animal-based foods, and provitamin A or beta-carotene, found in colorful fruits like yellow, and some types of green fruits and vegetables.

How to naturally increase iron levels?

You can increase your iron levels by making the right food choices. Heme iron comes from animal sources and is found in all types of meat, while non-heme iron is found in plant-based sources like fruits, vegetables, grains, legumes, and seeds. Be sure to include foods like spinach, lentils, beans, hemp seeds, and others.

The five most important antioxidants

Antioxidants are molecules that fight free radicals in the body. The 5 most important antioxidants are: Vitamin C, Vitamin E, Vitamin A, Zinc, and Selenium.

The healthiest winter vegetables

Winter vegetables can survive at very low temperatures thanks to their high content of fiber, vitamins, minerals, and other nutrients. Some of the most practical for use during the winter are: Brussels sprouts, kale, winter vegetables are potatoes, among this time of year, kale, Brussels sprouts, carrots, parsnips, turnips, red colored greens, and red cabbage.

What is the clean-eating concept?

The clean eating concept involves food that is unaltered for consumption, rich in nutrients and is rich in minerals and vitamins in its most natural form. This includes fresh fruits and vegetables, nuts, legumes, whole grains, plant-based and/or animal protein, seeds, nuts, and other minimally processed natural foods.

Is frozen vegetables as healthy as fresh ones?

From the moment fruits and vegetables are harvested, their quality begins to decrease and as a result, they slowly start to lose their nutritional value. Since it's widely known that frozen foods maintain their nutritional value for a longer period, it is important to know that frozen foods are just as nutritious as fresh ones that you can find in supermarkets and at markets.
